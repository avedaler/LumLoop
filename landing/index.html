FAQ

Common questions.

What makes LumLoop different from WHOOP or Oura?

WHOOP and Oura are excellent at measuring biometric data, but they stop at measurement. LumLoop takes that data — along with biomarker results, supplement protocols, and nutrition — and closes the loop by recommending, fulfilling, and then measuring whether your interventions actually worked. It's the difference between a dashboard and an operating system.

What is Biological Wellness Age?

It's a composite score derived from five biological subsystems — cardiovascular, sleep, metabolic, immune, and muscular — based on your biomarker data and daily health metrics. Unlike chronological age, it changes based on your interventions. It's your measurable north star for whether your wellness protocols are working.

Do I need to buy supplements through LumLoop?

No. LumLoop recommends personalized supplement protocols based on your data. You can source them anywhere. But our integrated marketplace makes it effortless — one tap to order exactly what your protocol requires, from verified suppliers.

How does the AI wellness coach work?

The coach has full context on your biomarkers, supplement stack, sleep data, and wellness goals. It generates evidence-based, personalized guidance — not generic responses. Ask it anything: why a supplement was recommended, how to improve your sleep score, what to eat before a workout.

What markets is LumLoop available in?

We're launching in Singapore, Malaysia, and the UAE — markets with high-income professionals who invest significantly in health optimization. Expansion to additional markets is planned for 2027.

Is my health data private?

Absolutely. All health data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell user data. You own your data and can export or delete it at any time. We comply with PDPA (Singapore), PDPA (Malaysia), and relevant UAE data protection regulations.
